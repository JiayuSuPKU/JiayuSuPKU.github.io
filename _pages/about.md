I am currently a senior college student at Peking University double majoring in Biological Sciences and Mathematics. While conducting my undergraduate research on statistical modeling of single-cell multi-omics under the supervision of [Professor Cheng Li](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/?term=cheng_li@pku.edu.cn), I am broadly interested in applying and developing innovative genome technologies and data mining methods to biomedical topics related to diseases and potential therapeutic strategies.

I was an international Student-At-Large (2018) at University of Chicago, and a summer research assistant (2019) at Harvard Medical School supervised by [Professor Hongbo Luo](http://www.childrenshospital.org/Research/Labs/luo-laboratory).
