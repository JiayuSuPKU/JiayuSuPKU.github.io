I am currently a senior college student at Peking University double majoring in Biological Sciences and Mathematics. While conducting my undergraduate research on statistical modeling of single-cell multi-omics under the supervision of [Professor Cheng Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=nJd3cIsAAAAJ&hl=en), I am broadly interested in applying and developing innovative genome technologies and data mining methods to biomedical topics related to diseases and potential therapeutic strategies.

I am also a member of the [Undergraduate Honors Program in Biology (UHPB)](http://web.bio.pku.edu.cn/UHPB/) at Peking University.

I was an international Student-At-Large (2018) at University of Chicago, and a summer research assistant (2019) at Harvard Medical School supervised by [Professor Hongbo Luo](http://www.childrenshospital.org/Research/Labs/luo-laboratory).
